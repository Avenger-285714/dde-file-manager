Detailed Description

DFMCrumbManager is the manager of crumb controllers

DFMCrumbManager is the manager of crumb controllers, it holds all avaliable crumb controllers to do crumb bar reletive stuff.

User can also write plugins to extend crumb bar functionality (by derive the DFMCrumbInterface class) and DFMCrumbManager do the job about load and manage the plugins.

See also DFMCrumbBar and DFMCrumbInterface.
